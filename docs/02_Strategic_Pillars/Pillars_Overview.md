# ReloNL Strategic Pillars

ReloNL is built on four strategic pillars that address the specific needs of international students, universities, and landlords in the Dutch market.

## 🏛️ The Four Pillars

| Pillar | Focus | Value Proposition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Student Excellence** | Solvency & BSN | Helping students become "Verified Tenants" and breaking the BSN loop. |
| **2. Growth & Rewards** | Referral System | A token-based economy where students earn credits for helping others. |
| **3. Institutional Trust** | University Integration | Offering white-label housing modules to universities like TU/e and TU Delft. |
| **4. Landlord Shield** | Legal Compliance | Protecting landlords from fines through the WWSO calculator and "Uitponden" protection. |
| **5. Future Growth & Innovation** | AI-Powered "Gems" & Partnerships | Integrating advanced AI features and leveraging the OfficeAIM ecosystem for enhanced user value. |

## 🛡️ Ethics & Transparency
Unlike previous failed platforms (e.g., Studently.nl), ReloNL:
- **Never** charges illegal student-facing intermediary fees.
- **Monetizes** via B2B subscriptions from property management firms.
- **Maintains** transparency through the "Espresso Test" (SEPA QR trust signal).
