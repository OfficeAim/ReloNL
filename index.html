⚖️ Legal Coach

Can my landlord refuse to register me at the BRP?

No. Dutch law requires landlords to allow BRP registration. Refusal can be reported to the municipality.

What is the legal notice period for tenants?

For most student rentals: 1 month, ending on the first of the month. Check your contract for specifics.

Is a deposit over 2 months legal?

No. For furnished rentals, max 2 months. For unfurnished, max 2 months. Anything above is illegal.

What is the WWSO point system?

The Woningwaarderingsstelsel assigns points to a property based on size, quality, and energy label. Rent is capped accordingly.

Can a landlord charge key money?

No, "sleutelgeld" is illegal in the Netherlands. Report it to the Huurcommissie.
